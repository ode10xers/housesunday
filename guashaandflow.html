Express treatments

Gua sha + Flow facial

$135 - 45 Minutes

 

Let this zen and massage-focused facial transport you into a world of tranquility. This therapeutic facial is perfect for those that only prefer the holistic portion of a custom facial, which reduces puffiness, releases tension and leaving you feel relaxed, hydrated and lifted in a rested state. This is a wonderful same-day event-ready facial and can be done weekly.

This facial includes: cleansing ritual, light exfoliation, mini scalp soother, lymphatic drainage massage (face, neck, chest and shoulders), facial sculpting + Gua sha techniques and finishing product application using results-driven skincare.

*This treatment does not include extractions or a mask. For a full scale treatment, consider booking The Custom Advanced Treatment.

*Pregnancy safe

*For those experiencing active acne or reactive skin, an acupressure massage will take place of the massage portion or a different treatment be substituted.
